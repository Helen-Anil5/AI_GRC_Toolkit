A practical, interactive GRC toolkit designed to assess, map, and mitigate risks associated with enterprise AI systems. Aligned with the **NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)** and **ISO/IEC 42001:2023**.

## Project Objective
As AI adoption accelerates, organizations struggle to translate abstract AI ethics and regulatory concepts (like the EU AI Act) into actionable, auditable business controls. This toolkit bridges that gap by providing a standardized, automated method to evaluate AI use cases (e.g., HR Resume Screeners, Customer Service Chatbots).

## Features
- **Interactive Risk Calculator:** Weighted scoring engine evaluating Data Sensitivity, Autonomy, Impact, Fairness, and Explainability.
- **Framework Mapping:** Automatically maps identified risks to specific NIST AI RMF functions and ISO 42001 Annex A controls.
- **Pre-built Templates:** Includes a sample Risk Register and Controls Catalog tailored for a high-risk HR AI use case.
- **Professional Playbook:** Comprehensive documentation for enterprise deployment.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Streamlit (Interactive Web Dashboard)
- **Backend:** Python, Pandas, NumPy
- **Frameworks:** NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001
